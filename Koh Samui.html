3 best things to do in
Koh Samui

  • Beach Hop
  • Waterfall Hunting
  • Overlap Stone Viewpoint

If you’re looking for the top things to do in Koh Samui, we’ve put together the ultimate guide. After touring the whole island here are all the best spots.

1. Beach Hop

If you’re heading to the Thai paradise of Koh Samui then you’re probably going to be hunting for the best beaches on the island .Well we spent a few days driving around the whole island so that you don’t have to. From our personal favourite Silver Beach, to the lesser known haven of Maenam Beach, there are plenty!
And beach hopping will definitely be top of your things to do in Koh Samui list!

2. Waterfall Hunting"

A popular island activity, and another of the most popular things to do in Koh Samui is chase waterfalls.


There are a few other waterfalls scattered around the island but the most popular are Namuang Waterfalls 1 & 2 for a reason. The first being an easy walk down to a beautiful jumping rock, and the second a hike up to a beautiful viewpoint.

Overlap Stone View Point

There are a few man made viewpoints on the island but this rural one was our favourite! Overlap Stone is located at the back of an old Thai families home and it’s just a short walk through the jungle to get there.
Located just around the corner from Hin Ta & Hin Yai Rocks and Lamai Beach it’s definitely worth adding to your things to do in Koh Samui list if you’re in the area.
